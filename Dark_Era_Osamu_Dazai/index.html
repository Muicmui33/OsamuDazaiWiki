Osamu Dazai Dark Era

Osamu Dazai was 14-18 years old during the Dark Era and worked for the Port Mafia, and he was around 174 centimeters and weighed around 54 kilograms, which means he was significantly underweight, luckily, during the Agency Era, he gained some more weight, although still being quite underweight. And this is because Dazai doesn't, and didn't eat during this era either, too much. He could go several days without food, and usually would. He might stay up to three days without eating, as he saw eating as a chore and hunger was easy to subside. So, usually, he'd only eat one can of crabs and drink some sake every now and then.

The Dark Era started with Dazai attempting suicide at 14 (this is not shown in the series, but it is mentioned) and Ougai Mori finding him. Back at this time Mori was an underground mob doctor, and seeing Dazai he decided to take him in. After this Dazai started helping out a bit with the Port Mafia errands, and soon enough he was a Port Mafia executive, the youngest one in Port Mafia history. He was 16. And he was 15 when he met Chuuya Nakahara, who later on became his partner, and this marked the start of the Double Black (Soukoku in Japanese or as many people like to call it even while speaking English). Dazai really hates Chuuya though, yet they make an awesome duo when paired together. Once they took down an enitre enemy organization of the Port Mafia overnight.

Dazai was 14 when his (future/past) best friend Oda Sakunosuke found him. Dazai was at the brink of death after another suicide attempt and Oda decided to save him.

Dazai joined the Port Mafia in hopes of finding a reason to live, thinking that in the constant violence and death in the Port Mafia, ones soul touches the very essence of life, so he might find a reason to live from that. But he didn't. Which was why Oda Sakunosuke's dying wish was to have Dazai be on the side that saves people, since in the end it didn't matter to Dazai which he did. He still felt that cold emptiness simmer deep inside of him. But this was the option that Dazai was the most likely to not to regret did he ever truly manage to die.

Osamu Dazai wore a black trench coat, a black suit jacket, a white dress shirt along with black dress pants and a black tie. He also wears bandages so that they cover his almost entire body, neck, legs (except for that the legs aren't visible), arms, face (covering his right eye) and chest (which also isn't visible). He also has a bandaid on his left cheek.

Dazai is said to be extremely cold and ruthless during his time in the Port Mafia, and he left there such a mark, that still, even after years since he last was with the Port Mafia, they have a saying going around "The unfortune of Dazai's enemies is being his enemy in the first place". This is because of Dazai's intelligence and absolutely ruthless, immoral way of behaving towards the enemy.

Dazai's presonality during the Dark Era is said to be cold and emotionless, though he isn't emotionless. He just doesn't have the energy to give a fuck. He's a hardworking man, a complete opposite from his later Agency Era self. Even though he is cold and ruthless, sometimes depicited as heartless, he was still just a child forced to be view himself as a demon as seen with his title as the "Demon Prodigy of the Port Mafia". He still did have some humor and childishness in himself, even though he acted very mature for someone his age. He acted like an adult when he was just a teenager, and because of this he acts like a child when he's an adult.
