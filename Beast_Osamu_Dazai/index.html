BEAST Osamu Dazai

In the manga alternate universe of Bungo Stray Dogs named Bungo Stray Dogs:BEAST, Dazai stayed in the Pot Mafia since Oda Sakunosuke never joined the Port Mafia. In this alternate universe Dazai is the leader of the Port Mafia, meanwhile Mori is considered to have been assasinated by Mori, but was in fact saved by him. Dazai is still 22 years old in this alternate universe, just that he stayed in the Port Mafia and became the Port Mafia boss.

BEAST Dazai is the same 181 centimeters as in the original timeline, and of course he then weighs 67 kilograms too. Dazai's personality resembles closely the same he had during the original timeline in the Dark Era. So, he's cold, ruthless, but he does not romanticize death, and he still is a bit playful. Except he is much darker in this turn of events. Dazai did commit suicide, but it was more of a dark, tragic sacrifice for the success of a dreamy plan to save Oda Sakunosuke, who he had learned somehow from his original self was very important. And so he jumped off of a rooftop.

BEAST Dazai wears a black trench coat, a red scarf, a black tie, bandages all over his body, even covering his left eye, a black suit jacket, a white dress shirt and black dress pants. He wears a very similar attire to his original Dark Era self, except for that he's now wearing his bandage over his left eye, not his right. He's also wearing the scarf that Mori wears in the original timeline. This is used to enforce his role as the Port Mafia boss.

BEAST Dazai commited suicide in the last manga by jumping from a building after telling Atsushi Nakajima and Ryuonsuke Akutagawa the truth about The Book. The truth about all the different Alternate Universes, about how this was the only one where his dearest friend, who he never actually got to know in this one, was still alive and writing. But since no more than two at the same time could know about the truth or the universe would become unstable, and possibly cause Oda Sakunosuke to be unhappy, and so he jumped. He explained about how people would be coming to get The Book and try to change the universe, so he left the duty of protecting the city in Akutagawa's and Atsushi's hands.

BEAST Dazai tried meeting Oda Sakunosuke at least once himself right before his death, he tried being happy and to do what the other versions of himself had done with him. Drink and just talk about whatever. But Oda did not take lightly to this. Mostly because Dazai didn't explain anything too much. Oda even tried killing Dazai, but didn't when he begged not to be killed in that bar. Bar Lupin. This bar, in fact, actually exists in Japan, Tokyo.

BEAST Dazai called Oda Sakunosuke "Odasaku, like his original self did. But Oda, not knowing about the alternate universes, didn't like it, and said only his real friends were allowed to call him that. Dazai looked genuinely happy seeing Oda show up at that bar, despite Dazai never having to gotten know him in that universe. Either way, he seemed very heartbroken when Oda pointed the gun at him and threatened to kill him.
