Osamu Dazai Agency Era

Osamu Dazai is a side character in the anime and manga series Bungo Stray Dogs. He's 22 years old (current Agency era time), weighs around 67 kilograms and is 181 centimeters. )

Dazai's likes include: Oda Sakunosuke, sake, whiskey, crab, suicide, death and cats.

Dazai's dislikes include: Dogs, Nakahara Chuuya, people who are extremely arrogant but lack the intelligence to back it up, being controlled or used.

Dazai's personality during the Agency Era is extremely childish and lazy. He's constantly joking and hiding his emotions and despair behind a mask of humor. But he is also constantly seen pushing the other members of the Armed Detective Agency to their potential, even if he avoids work, and especially physical labour himself. And this is seen for example in episode 12 season 1 when Dazai tells Atsushi to do his report from when he was kidnapped by the Port Mafia for him.

Dazai is often depicited as a manipulative and enigmatic person in the anime and manga series Bungo Stray Dogs. He is extremely intelligent and is not afraid to use his intelligence.

Dazai is really depressed and suicidal, and while he exhibits clear signs of this, no one really knows how far this loneliness and deep nihilism is stretching. Oda Sakunosuke who was Dazai's closest friend during the Dark Era, said that no matter where Dazai was, his loniless could never be filled. Oda Sakunosuke was the closest to understanding Dazai's feelings. Now, we see Dazai joking about suicide or double suicide and constantly trying to to commit suicide or find a partner, preferably a beautiful woan, to join him in a double suicide.

Dazai is seen as a mentor figure by Ryunsuke Akutagawa and Atsushi Nakajima. With Atsushi being the current mentee of Dazai. Akutagawa, being Dazai's former mentee from back when Dazai was with the Port Mafia during the Datk Era, was in an extremely toxic relationship with him, leading to trauma bonding. And Dazai just lets it be, even as he promised to be on the side that saves people. He still uses extremely dark morality and manipulation. In other words, Dazai is quite amoral.

The character Osamu Dazai is based off of the author Osamu Dazai(1909-1948). And this author Dazai, he was also deeply suicidal and depressed, viewing himself as "no longer human". Therefore, he wrote the book No Longer Human(1948), which the character Dazai is further based on. This author and his book are exactly what have inspired the character Osamu Dazai. As in the world of Bungo Stray Dogs there exist "Abilities", but only in a certain chosen amount of individuals. Dazai, being one of them, has the ability named No Longer Human, after the book. His ability allows him to nullify anyone else's ability, no matter what it is.
